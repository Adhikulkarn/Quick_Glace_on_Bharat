QUICK GLANCE ABOUT BHARAT

Its a hand written collection

Motto: Sathyameva Jayathe

The main motto of this project is ‘Sathyameva Jayathe’.

This project, named “QUICK GLANCE ABOUT BHARAT,” was launched on May 2nd, 2024, driven by the motivation to preserve India's heritage, much like the ancient manuscripts of Bharat. These handwritten records embody the nation's identity by documenting a vast array of subjects, from the Vedas and Upanishads to epics like the Ramayana and Mahabharata, Ayurveda, and various art forms.

The project aims to provide a comprehensive and verified repository of information about India, including famous personalities, notable places, and museums etc. A key element of its uniqueness is the rigorous verification process: all information is meticulously authenticated and officially sealed with the signatures of the relevant departments and individuals.

This initiative seeks to be a modern-day manuscript, making India’s rich knowledge, culture, and wisdom accessible and trustworthy for future generations.
